# Coosto - Company Research

## What They Do
Content & social media marketing SaaS platform. Helps organizations create, schedule, publish, and analyze social content across multiple channels. 15,000+ users globally.

## Product Modules
1. **Listen** - Online media monitoring (brand/industry coverage, 150 languages, 200 countries)
2. **Engage** - All-in-one social media inbox (interactions, messengers, webcare)
3. **Publish** - Smart social media planner (scheduling, optimization)
4. **Report** - Intelligent reporting/dashboards/analytics
5. **Discover** - AI-powered content discovery (trending topics)

## Recent Launch
- **EmotionAI** - Reputation measured as emotional profiles: sarcasm detection, emotion/intensity shifts over time, per brand/product name analysis. Maarten posted about this proudly on LinkedIn.

## Key Integrations
Salesforce, ChatGPT, Google Analytics, EmotionAI

## Career & Team Culture (from HR call + 2nd interview)
- **Career growth program:** Structured career development with clear visibility into your progress and growth path within the company
- **Mentorship:** Some kind of mentorship program for employees
- **Training budget:** ~1K EUR per person, never actually declined a course. Can expand if justified. Conferences, Udemy courses encouraged
- **Internal Talks:** Knowledge sharing sessions
- **Guilds:** Frontend Guild, Backend Guild, etc. — cross-team knowledge sharing
- **Team building:** Summer BBQ in Holland, collaborative party, Christmas party, activities
- **Team building trips:** Alicante team goes to Netherlands a couple times/year + Netherlands team comes to Alicante periodically
- **Office policy:** Everything online but Thursday = office/team day. At least 2 days at office, maybe 3. Flexible
- **Career mobility:** People move between departments (junior → senior → different role → junior → senior again)
- **Longevity:** People stay 10-15 years (Maarten ~10 years, Pim ~12 years). They genuinely love working there
- **Collaborative culture:** Opinions on product improvements are valued. Can help outside your role
